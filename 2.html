Fishmatic

Fishmatic is an application developed to aid your fishing experience. Fishmatic aims to provide relevant information for your fishing experiences based on user inputted database.

Your input matters and it is totally up to you should you choose to share your records to fellow anglers on Fishmatic or to keep your records private.

Fishmatic is developed for the fellow anglers in goodwill and is free to use personally.
